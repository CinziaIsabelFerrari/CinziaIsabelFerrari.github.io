Hey! I'm Cinzia!

I am an Italian design researcher. Multisciplinarity and the speculation of novel solutions through cross-domain breeding have been central to my experience combining biology, design and technology. My research interests are centred around the exploration of bio-inspired systems that have the potential to dissolve the boundaries between natural, human and digital worlds.

I operate comfortably in areas that are unknown, bridging across disciplines in search for better futures for todays' problems- through design innovation.
